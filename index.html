How It Works

Our platform makes it easy for restaurants, grocery stores, and food vendors to donate surplus food to those in need. Here’s how it works:

Step 1: Sign Up

Join Eco Eats by signing up on our platform. It's free and easy to get started.

Step 2: Donating or Selling Surplus Food

List the food you want to sell or donate, and we'll connect you with a users or NGOs in need.

Step 3: Pickup and Delivery

We handle the logistics. Our team picks up the food from your location and delivers it to the community.
